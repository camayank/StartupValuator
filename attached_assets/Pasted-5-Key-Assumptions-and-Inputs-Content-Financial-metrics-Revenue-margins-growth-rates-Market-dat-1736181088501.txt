Content:
Final valuation figure, broken down by method.

Sensitivity analysis showing variable impacts.
Visuals:
Tornado diagram, sensitivity curves, and bar graphs.

Content:
Identified risks (e.g., market volatility, regulatory hurdles).
Impact of risks on valuation.
Suggested mitigation strategies.
Visuals:
Radar chart showing risk profile.
8. Compliance and Regional Adjustments
Content:
Compliance standards followed (e.g., ICAI, 409A).
Regional assumptions (e.g., inflation rates, currency adjustments).
Visuals:
Highlight regional factors in a table or graphic.

Glossary of financial and valuation terms.
Visuals:
Use plain text or static tables for supporting data.
