Here are **25 significant industries** along with their **key valuation parameters** that can be used in valuation calculations:

---

### 1. **SaaS (Software as a Service)**
   - Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)/Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)
   - Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
   - Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)
   - Churn Rate
   - Gross Retention Rate

### 2. **E-commerce**
   - Gross Merchandise Value (GMV)
   - Average Order Value (AOV)
   - Customer Retention Rate
   - Inventory Turnover
   - Return Rate

### 3. **Manufacturing**
   - Asset Utilization
   - Operating Margin
   - Working Capital Efficiency
   - Return on Invested Capital (ROIC)
   - Capacity Utilization

### 4. **Real Estate**
   - Net Operating Income (NOI)
   - Capitalization Rate (Cap Rate)
   - Occupancy Rate
   - Asset Value
   - Loan-to-Value (LTV) Ratio

### 5. **Healthcare**
   - Patient Lifetime Value
   - Revenue per Patient
   - Regulatory Compliance Costs
   - Average Treatment Cost
   - Operating Margins

### 6. **Renewable Energy**
   - Energy Output (kWh)
   - Carbon Credit Value
   - ROI (Return on Investment)
   - Levelized Cost of Energy (LCOE)
   - ESG Metrics

### 7. **Banking and Financial Services**
   - Net Interest Margin (NIM)
   - Loan-to-Deposit Ratio
   - Cost of Funds
   - Non-Performing Assets (NPA) Ratio
   - Return on Assets (ROA)

### 8. **Hospitality**
   - Average Daily Rate (ADR)
   - Revenue per Available Room (RevPAR)
   - Occupancy Rate
   - Customer Satisfaction Index
   - Loyalty Program Impact

### 9. **Education and EdTech**
   - Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
   - Lifetime Value of Learners (LTV)
   - Retention Rate
   - Number of Paid Subscribers
   - Course Completion Rate

### 10. **Retail**
   - Sales per Square Foot
   - Inventory Turnover
   - Customer Retention Rate
   - Average Basket Size
   - Gross Margin Return on Investment (GMROI)

### 11. **Logistics and Supply Chain**
   - Cost per Mile
   - Delivery Accuracy Rate
   - Warehouse Utilization Rate
   - Operating Cost per Delivery
   - Inventory Carrying Cost

### 12. **Media and Entertainment**
   - Revenue per User (RPU)
   - Advertising Revenue
   - Subscriber Growth Rate
   - Average Time Spent
   - Content Production Costs

### 13. **Telecommunications**
   - Average Revenue per User (ARPU)
   - Churn Rate
   - Spectrum Utilization
   - Network Downtime
   - Subscriber Growth

### 14. **Pharmaceuticals**
   - R&D Expenditure
   - Drug Pipeline Value
   - Regulatory Approval Rates
   - Market Exclusivity Period
   - Gross Profit Margin

### 15. **Agriculture and Agritech**
   - Yield per Acre
   - Input Costs (e.g., fertilizers, seeds)
   - Crop Cycle Time
   - Market Price of Produce
   - Storage Costs

### 16. **Food and Beverage**
   - Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)
   - Revenue per Outlet
   - Customer Retention Rate
   - Average Order Value
   - Gross Margin

### 17. **Automotive**
   - Units Sold
   - Market Share
   - Cost per Vehicle
   - Supply Chain Costs
   - Warranty Claim Costs

### 18. **Technology Hardware**
   - Units Shipped
   - R&D Spend
   - Gross Profit Margin
   - Inventory Turnover
   - Average Selling Price (ASP)

### 19. **Oil and Gas**
   - Barrel per Day Production
   - Reserve Replacement Ratio
   - Operating Cost per Barrel
   - Refining Margins
   - ESG Metrics

### 20. **Construction and Infrastructure**
   - Project Backlog
   - Cost Overrun Ratio
   - Revenue per Project
   - Gross Profit Margin
   - On-time Delivery Rate

### 21. **Fintech**
   - Transaction Volume
   - Average Revenue per User (ARPU)
   - Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
   - Retention Rate
   - Total Payment Value (TPV)

### 22. **Gaming**
   - Daily Active Users (DAU)
   - Monthly Active Users (MAU)
   - Average Revenue per User (ARPU)
   - Time Spent per User
   - In-app Purchase Revenue

### 23. **Textile and Apparel**
   - Cost per Unit
   - Inventory Turnover
   - Market Penetration
   - Average Selling Price (ASP)
   - Return Rate

### 24. **Aerospace and Defense**
   - Order Backlog
   - Cost per Unit Produced
   - Contract Win Rate
   - Research and Development (R&D) Costs
   - Operating Margin

### 25. **Utilities**
   - Energy Delivered (MWh)
   - Cost per Unit of Energy
   - Distribution Efficiency
   - Renewable Energy Share
   - Customer Retention Rate
