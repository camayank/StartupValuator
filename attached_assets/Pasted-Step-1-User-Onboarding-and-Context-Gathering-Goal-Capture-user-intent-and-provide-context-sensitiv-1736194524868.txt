Step 2: Business Basics (General Information)
Goal: Collect foundational data for tailored valuation logic.
Prompts:
"What is the name of your business?"
"Select your industry sector." (Dropdown: SaaS, Manufacturing, Retail, etc.)
"What is the primary region of operation?" (Dropdown: US, India, UK, etc.)
"What is the purpose of this valuation?" (Fundraising, Exit, Internal Review)
Step 3: Financial Data Collection
Goal: Collect core quantitative inputs with minimal friction.
Prompts:
"What is your annual revenue (or projected revenue for the next year)?"
"What is your expected revenue growth rate over the next 5 years?" (Provide slider for ease of use)
"Enter your current operating margin (percentage)." (With a tooltip: How to calculate this)
Step 4: Market-Specific Inputs
Goal: Capture external and qualitative inputs for benchmarking and risk assessment.
Prompts:
"What is the size of your total addressable market (TAM)?" (AI can suggest benchmarks based on industry)
"Who are your key competitors?" (Optional text input, or select from suggestions)
"How scalable is your business model on a scale of 1-10?"
Step 5: Risk Assessment and Qualitative Data
Goal: Integrate subjective elements to refine valuation logic.
Prompts:
"What is the primary risk facing your business?" (Market, Operational, Regulatory)
"How stable is your cash flow?" (Stable, Moderate, Volatile)
"Do you have any intellectual property protection in place?" (Yes/No/Not Applicable)
Step 6: Jurisdiction-Specific Adjustments
Goal: Apply region-specific compliance standards and assumptions.
Prompts:
"Does your business comply with ICAI valuation standards (India)?" (Yes/No)
"Is this valuation 409A-compliant (US-based ESOP valuations)?" (Yes/No)
"Do you need IFRS-based adjustments (Europe)?"
Step 7: Valuation Review and Report Customization
Goal: Provide users with control over assumptions and report format.
Prompts:
"Review these assumptions. Would you like to adjust any key values?" (Interactive table)
"What format would you like your valuation report in?" (PDF, Excel, Branded PDF)
"Would you like to include scenario analysis or sensitivity curves in the report?"
