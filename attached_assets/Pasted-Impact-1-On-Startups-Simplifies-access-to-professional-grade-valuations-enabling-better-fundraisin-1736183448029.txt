Impact
1. On Startups
Simplifies access to professional-grade valuations, enabling better fundraising and strategic decision-making.
Reduces costs for early-stage companies with limited budgets.
2. On Valuers and Financial Professionals
Automates time-consuming calculations and compliance checks.
Serves as a reliable tool for baseline valuations, allowing them to focus on nuanced adjustments.
3. On Investors
Provides faster, more accurate valuations for due diligence.
Offers a unified platform for managing portfolios and assessing investment opportunities.
4. On the Ecosystem
Improves transparency and standardization in startup valuations.
Empowers underserved markets with access to global valuation standards.
